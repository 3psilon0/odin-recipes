The Forbidden Method

This method of making tea is quick, easy and is sure to impress all of your tea-loving friends!

Things You Need:

  • A cup of water
  • A teabag
  • A Microwave

Steps:

  1. Put the cup of water inside the microwave
  2. Microwave the cup of water till it boils
  3. Put the teabag in the cup of (hot) water and wait for it to brew
