The Cool Method

literally...

This type of tea is made not by steeping your choice of tea in hot water but rather in cold water.
It is a delightful drink to guzzle down on a hot summer day (or winter day idfk i wont judge you)

Things you will need:

  • H2O
  • Some tea of your choice
  • A covered container to steep your tea in
  • A refrigerator

Instructions:

  1. Put your tea in the container, you can put as much or as little tea as you would like depending on how strong you like your tea
  2. Fill your container with water
  3. leave the container in the fridge for about 8-12 hours
  4. Thats it
