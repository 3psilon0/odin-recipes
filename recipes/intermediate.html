The Normie Method

Tea.

Things You Need:

  • A cup of water
  • A container you can boil water in
  • A tea bag

How to do it:

  1. Put the water in the container of your choice
  2. Put the container on a lit stove and wait for the water to boil
  3. Put the teabag inside the cup
  4. When the water has come to a boil, pour it in the cup and wait for the tea to brew
  5. Good job! You have made tea
